SPAGETTI

Ingredients

  1. Spagetti
  2. onions
  3. tomatos
  4. carrots
  5. galic
  6. green papper
  7. salt
  8. oil
  9. cury powder

STEPS

1.Slice or blend your ingredients and put them a side.
2.In a pan,place your spagetti sticks together with garlic,little salt and water.When ready, filter exess water out.
3. In another pan, put oil til ready
Then put the onions ,carrots, green papper and let them cook .
Then put in your tomatoes and let them cook.
4. When done add in ur other ingredients like black papper ,rayco and salt.
5. Then mix your spagetti with the ingredients and thats how its done.
Congradulations on cooking delicious spagetti.

KATOGO

Ingredients

  1. matooke
  2. meat
  3. tomatoes
  4. onions
  5. green papper
  6. salt
  7. cury powder, black papper and other spices

STEPS

1. Peal the matooke ,wash the meat and all ingredients .
2. Slice or blend ingredients to desiered shape and cut meat into small pieces.
3.In a pan place your meat and put it on fire ,let it cook and create its oil .
Add other ingredients in intervals exept salt and the cury.
4.Put the matoke in and add water to it and let it boil on medium heat.
5. When the matoke is ready it turns yellow and then add your saly and other spices with little water to the mixture.
Let it boil for some minutes and then its ready for serving.

BEEF STEW


Ingredients

  1. Beef
  2. onions
  3. tomatos
  4. carrots
  5. ginger
  6. galic
  7. green papper
  8. salt
  9. oil
  10. cury powder

STEPS

1.Wash and slice the beef into small pieces.
2.In a pan place your beef with ginger, garlic and salt with some water and put on fire to boil.
3.When the water dries out mix the beef until it makes its oil ,then place in your other ingredients.
4. Mix them together and for a thick soup u can add some corn starch or wheat flour.
5. Let it boil till ready and ready to be served with any desiered food.

SPANISH OMELETTE

Ingredients

  1. 3 eggs
  2. 2 or 3 potateos
  3. onions
  4. any spices like black paper
  5. salt
  6. oil
  7. green papper , carots

STEPS

1. Peal and wash your potateos together with your ingredients.
2. Slice your potateos and ingredients into small pieces and in a pan pour some oil and give them small burn.
3. In a bowl break three eggs and add a pince of salt and your preffered spices and mix it together and properly.
4. Mix your ingredients you had cooked before and mix it properly.
5. In a clean pan,with low heat,pour some oil and spread it on the pan.Pour your egg mixture in and spread it on the pan then cover with and leave to cook for some minutes till the lower part is brown.
6. With a help of a plate fleap the omelette to the other side to cook.Add any source of ur choice and enjoy.

CHAPATTI

ingredients

  1. Wheat flour
  2. blended carots, onions and green paper
  3. salt
  4. oil
  5. warm water
  6. milk
  7. eggs

STEPS

1. In a dish mix your liquid ingredients eg eggs, milk together with blended carots, onions and green papper.
2. In another dish pour your flour and salt. then mix the two together while kneding.
3. When the dough is ready it will stop sticking on your hands. 4. Then rub some oil on it and cover,let it settle for some time.
5. After it has swollen, make small pinches and kneed them to get a round shape.
6. Then fry the chapati with oil on a hot pan. while placing them in a foil to maintain their moisture and heat.
The chapatis are ready and serve with preffered source.
