WELCOME TO RECIPE BOOK

The Ugandan meals are spicy and exotic in taste.The basic ingredients in ugandan cooking is plantains,cassava,and other vegetables like yams.
A variety of locally available ingredients like tomatos,onions,carrots,spinach,ginger and other spices are used to make food flavorful.

The Ugandan cooking generally revolves around native cerals,beans,grains and other indiginously available fruits and vegetables ,milk and milk products.
The dishes and food preferences also show a versatility according to different regions.
